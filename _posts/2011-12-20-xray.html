Xray is a handy little Javascript tool for web developers. It allows you to "X-ray" any webpage so you can see positions, margins, padding, dimension, and extra details of any HTML element. The great thing about it that it costs...nothing! It's free! It's not even a download! All you need to do is drag this bookmark to your bookmarks bar. When you need it, you just click on the bookmark and you'll be presented with a nice little welcome screen:

You can click on any element to X-ray it:



All in all, it's a really great little tool. I would highly recommend it for anyone doing web development work.
